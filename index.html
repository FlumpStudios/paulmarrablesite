About Me

I am currently a director of engieering for Electronic Arts Southam. I also run an independent video game studio called Flump Studios and previously co-owned a small web design company called Plinx.

I have contributed to 8 AAA games, including F1 2020-2024, Grid Legends, and Dirt 5. Additionally, I provided post-release support for Dirt Rally 1 and 2.

As the founder and developer of Flump Studios, I have independently released 13 games, including notable titles such as Horizon Shift, Horizon Shift 81, Hyper Bounce Blast, and Super Killer Hornet Resurrection.

Over the past few years my professional career has mainly been focused on the racing companion app Racenet. Where I orginally led the backend development team as well as handling the visualisations for the F1 and WRC performance analysis tool. The Racenet service also provideds additional server support for the games, such as hosting and running the F1 leagues and WRC clubs features.

Recently we have switched focus to unannoucned project where I am leading a team of 11 enginners to create something pretty unique (that's all I can say).

I have been developing software and games for over 30 years and I have maintained a strong passion for software development. Over the years I have gained experience developing with many different languages and frameworks including... C, C++, C#, Javascript, Python, VB, Lua, Unreal(4 and 5) Unity 3D, HTML, CSS, SASS, AngularJS, ReactJS, ASP.Net, SQL, NoSql (Mongo mainly) and various other languages and frameworks. (C will always be my first love)

Within my current role I tend to work server side and my prefered stack is .NET 8 with EF and postgres. I also dabble in the front end, but this tends to be more around 3D rendering and visualisations, for this I like to use react with react three fiber. Some parts of my role require me to work within Unreal 4 and Unity3D.

As an indie game developer I have released games in 6502, C/C++, VB, Construct 2/3, Unity3D, XNA, Monogame. For my current project I am writring a custom engine in C with Raylib for engine rendering. The engine exports to a custom raycasting engine (based of anarch and SDL), Unreal 5 and OpenGl(For future dreamcast compatibility). I am also working on a series of free platform games called X-Ninja with my 7 year old son.

I live in Leamington Spa with my wife and 2 sons, I enjoy skateboarding, retro console programming, and running.
